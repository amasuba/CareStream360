Core Philosophy

Key Innovation

From KPI to KQE Approach

Traditional KPI Model

Focuses on technical performance metrics like latency, throughput, and error rates without considering actual user experience

New KQE Model

Combines technical KPIs with behavioral data to measure actual Quality of Experience from the customer's perspective

KQE = Key Quality of Experience

The measure that truly matters to customers and business success
